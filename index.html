Internship at Laboratoire Adhesion et Inflammation (LAI)
Software development for Steered Molecular Dynamics (SMD) simulations analysis

The Steered Molecular Dynamics (SMD) method was developed to mimic Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) pulling experiments, where external forces are applied to probe biomolecules in addition to their intrinsic forces defined in classical MD simulations. SMD simulations have been widely used to explore the mechanics of biomolecular processes such as unbinding and unfolding at the single-molecule level. The goal of the project was to develop a software package for SMD simulations analysis. The package had to include a Graphical User Interface (GUI).
